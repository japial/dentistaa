Frequently Asked Questions

Who is a BMDC recognized dentist?

A BMDC recognized dentist, is a dental surgeon who is graduated with BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) degree from any govt. recognized dental college/medical college dental unit and got the license from the BMDC for clinical practice.

What is BMDC?

The Bangladesh Medical & Dental Council (BMDC) is a statutory body with the responsibility of establishing and maintaining high standards of medical education and recognition of medical qualifications in Bangladesh. It registers doctors to practice in Bangladesh, in order to protect and promote the health and safety of the public by ensuring proper standards in the practice of medicine.

What do I need to bring to my first appointment?

Please bring the following items with you to your appointment:

  1. Patient Information Form (if applicable)
  2. Patient Health History Form (if any)
  3. Dental Insurance Card (if applicable)
  4. Patient Health History Form (if any)
  5. Prescription from other physicians. (if any)

How long it takes to confirm my appointment?

Maximum 30 minutes. We will notify you by after confirmation.

Can I change my appointment time/date?

You can change once, before 1 hour of scheduled time.

Do I need to arrive early for my first appointment?

Yes. Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to fill out any remaining patient forms

What should I do if I require premedication?

Please be sure to request a prescription prior to your appointment, or if you are unsure, contact us and we can help.

Why should I bring Prescription from other physicians?

It helps your dentist to understand the underlying conditions & causes of your illness. And to avoid any adverse drug interaction.

How long will my first appointment last?

It varies, but please plans on 1 to 1.5 hours for the first visit.
